## Optifade Concealment Technology

Developed in partnership with W.L. Gore, Optifade is the only concealment pattern designed around how animals see. The Elevated II pattern is specifically engineered for treestand hunting.

- **GORE-TEX INFINIUM** - Windproof and highly breathable protection
- **Micro-Fleece Interior** - Quiet, comfortable insulation
- **Articulated Patterning** - Full range of motion for drawing your bow
- **Low-Profile Collar** - Won't interfere with your bowstring
