Our Story

BOLINGO was founded with a simple mission: to connect hunters with the best locations, knowledge, and gear to make every hunt successful. Based in Hampton, Virginia, we understand the unique hunting opportunities and challenges of the Mid-Atlantic region.

Our team consists of lifelong hunters, conservation enthusiasts, and outdoor adventurers who believe in preserving the hunting tradition while embracing modern tools and techniques. We're not just a directory or a store - we're a community dedicated to helping hunters of all experience levels succeed.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Directory: Our curated database of hunting locations includes public lands, private properties, and wildlife management areas throughout Virginia and neighboring states. Each listing is vetted and includes detailed information about access, species, and regulations.
  • Expert Content: From beginner guides to advanced techniques, our blog features articles written by experienced hunters who know these lands. We cover everything from gear reviews to hunting strategies to conservation efforts.
  • Quality Gear: Our store features hand-selected products that we trust and use ourselves. We only recommend gear that meets our standards for quality, durability, and value.

Our Values

Conservation First: We believe in responsible hunting that supports healthy wildlife populations and preserves natural habitats for future generations.

Community: Hunting is more than a hobby - it's a tradition that brings people together. We foster a welcoming community for hunters of all backgrounds and experience levels.

Transparency: We're honest about our recommendations and clear about our partnerships. When we review gear or recommend a location, you can trust that it's based on real experience.

The Hampton, VA Region

The Hampton Roads area offers incredible diversity for hunters. From the waterfowl-rich Chesapeake Bay to the deer-filled forests of the interior, our region provides year-round hunting opportunities. We're proud to call this area home and to share our knowledge of its best hunting spots.

Join Us

Whether you're a seasoned hunter looking for new spots or a newcomer ready to start your hunting journey, BOLINGO is here to help. Explore our directory, read our guides, and gear up for your next adventure.
